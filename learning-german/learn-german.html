Table of grammar

I must drink.Ich muss trinken. I must to-drink.
I want to drink.Ich möchte trinken.I want to-drink.
I’m going to drink tomorrow.Ich werde morgen trinken.I going tomorrow to-drink.
I can’t drink.Ich kann nicht trinken.I can not to-drink.
The wine is red.Der Wein ist rot.The wine is red.
It is Ulrich’s wine.Er ist Ulrichs Wein.He is Ulrich’s wine.
I give Tia the wine.Ich gebe Tia den Wein.I give Tia the wine.
We give her the wine.Wir geben ihr den Wein.We give her the wine.
He gives it to Tia.Er gibt ihn Tia.He gives him Tia.
She gives it to him.Sie gibt ihn ihm.She gives him him.
Is the wine red?Ist der Wein rot?Is the wine red?
The wines are red.Die Weine sind rot.The wines are red.
I must give it to him.Ich muss ihn ihm geben.I must him him to-give.
I want to give it to her.I will ihn ihr geben.I want him her to-give.
I have drunk the wine.Ich habe den Wein getrunken.I have the wine drunk.

Learning process á la Tim Ferriss

For eight weeks we have:

  1. Sugar high after 1 week out of 8 weeks
  2. Followed by immediate drop and low point after 2 weeks
  3. Rapid progress after the low point, followed by plateau after 3 weeks
  4. Inflection point at after 6 weeks
  5. Fluency after 8 weeks

Even if you are not 100% fluent, you still win with 50%!
